David Schomer and the development of Latte Art in America

A Rough Timeline of the History of Latte Art

  • 1980's and 1990's - Latte Art is popularized in the United States by David Schomer
  • 1986 - Jack Kelly of Uptown Espresso develops techniques for milk texturing
  • 1989 - The Heart pattern is a signature at David Schomer's Espresso Vivace
  • 1992 - Schomer develops the Rosette pattern, based on photos from Cafe Mateki in Italy
